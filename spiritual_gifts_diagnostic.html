How to Use This Diagnostic

A spiritual gift is a special quality given by the Holy Spirit to every believer for the benefit of the whole church (1 Corinthians 12:4–7). This diagnostic helps you discover how God has wired you — not so you know what to avoid, but so you know where to begin.

Answer each statement honestly based on how you actually are — not how you wish you were or how you think a good Christian should answer. The most useful results come from the most honest responses.
