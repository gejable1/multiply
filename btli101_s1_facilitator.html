🎯 Intern Pre-Session Briefing — 15 min before
Gather all three interns. Cover: (1) The main truth of today and why it matters for your people. (2) Walk through the PUKAW activity physically — they need to have done it themselves before facilitating it. (3) The one moment in TALAKAY not to rush — the 4-minute silence. (4) Any pastoral concern carried over from orientation. Pray together. Send them to their tables.

Movement 1 · PUKAW
Engage — Create Felt Need
15 minutes · Session opens here
▼
The Activity
⚡ Pukaw Activity · Slides 1–3
Kilala Mo Ba Siya? (Do You Know Him?)
Setup: Project Slide 2. Each table has manila paper folded into two columns: "Alam ko TUNGKOL sa kanya" and "Kilala ko siya."

Round 1 (3 min): Think of a celebrity or public figure. Write 3–4 things you know ABOUT them in Column 1. Write what you actually know personally in Column 2. (Column 2 will be empty or near-empty — that's the point.)

Round 2 (2 min): Pairs share within the table. Expect laughter.

Round 3 — The Pivot (5 min): Switch to Slide 3. "Gawin nating muli — ngayon para sa Diyos." Individual writing, 90 seconds. No sharing yet — this is private.

Bridge (5 min): Use the script below to transition to TUKLAS.
Bridge Script
🎤 Your own words — taglish is natural here
"Ang karamihan sa atin — pati na ako — lumaki na may maraming alam TUNGKOL sa Diyos. Nadinig natin ang Kanyang pangalan sa simbahan. Sinabi natin ang Ama Namin. Alam natin ang mga kwento. Ngunit ang tanong ngayong araw — ang tanong na sasagutin ng buong BTLI 101 journey — ay ito: kilala mo ba Siya? Hindi kung alam mo ang tungkol sa Kanya. Kilala mo ba Siya — parang kaibigan? Parang Ama? Parang Persona na aktwal na nagmamahal sa iyo ngayon mismo? Iyan ang simula ng lahat. At iyan ang pag-uusapan natin ngayon."
🎯 Pastoral Note — What to Watch For
During Round 3, watch the faces as they fill Column 2 about God. Some write enthusiastically. Many pause and write very little. A few look uncomfortable — years of church attendance with no genuine personal connection. Do not call anyone out. See them. They are who this session is for.

Movement 2 · TUKLAS
Discover — Encounter the Truth
25 minutes · Guide discovery, do not lecture
▼
Scripture — Three Readings (Slide 4)
Jeremias 29:11–13 · Primary Text
"Sapagkat kilala Ko ang mga plano Ko para sa inyo — sabi ng Panginoon — mga planong magbibigay sa inyo ng pag-asa at kinabukasan, hindi kapahamakan. Pagkatapos, tatawag kayo sa Akin at magsusumamo sa Akin, at pakikinggan Ko kayo. Hahanapin ninyo Ako at mahahanap Ako, kapag hinahanap ninyo Ako nang buong puso."
"For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future. You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart."
Awit 139:1–6 · Supporting Text
"Panginoon, siniyasat Mo ako at kilala Mo ako. Alam Mo kung kailan ako umuupo at tumitindig; naiintindihan Mo ang aking mga iniisip mula sa malayo. Nababantayan Mo ang aking mga lakad at ang aking pahinga; pamilyar Ka sa lahat ng aking mga gawi."
"You have searched me, Lord, and you know me. You know when I sit and when I rise; you perceive my thoughts from afar. You are familiar with all my ways."
📖 Reading Method
Read Jeremiah in Tagalog — you first, slowly. One participant reads it in English. Everyone together in Tagalog. Then Psalm 139 — one participant reads it silently, then aloud. Three readings creates ownership without feeling repetitive.
Discovery Questions — Table Groups (Slide 5 · 12 min)
  • OBSERVATION: "Sa Jeremias 29:11–13 — ano ang tatlong bagay na ginagawa ng Diyos? Ilista sa manila paper." Guide toward: Knows the plans · Hears when we call · Is FOUND when sought with all the heart
  • INTERPRETATION: "Sa Awit 139 — anong uri ng pagkakilala mayroon ang Diyos sa atin? Hindi lang alam — kilala. Ano ang pagkakaiba?" Guide toward: Intimate personal knowledge — sitting, rising, thinking, speaking. He is familiar with ALL our ways.
  • APPLICATION: "Kung totoo ang lahat ng ito — paano nito binabago ang iyong pagtingin sa panalangin? Sa pagbabasa ng Biblia?" Guide toward: prayer becomes conversation, Bible reading becomes hearing from someone who actually knows you
Table Gallery Walk (optional if time allows)
📝 Table Activity
Sino ang Diyos para sa Akin?
Each table completes on manila paper: "Ang Diyos ay ___" — one sentence from what they discovered. Post on walls. 3-minute walk. Facilitator reads 2–3 aloud and affirms. Makes discovery visible and communal.
Bridge to TALAKAY
🎤 Draw out the main truth
"Napansin ko ang inyong mga sagot. Paulit-ulit nating naririnig ang isang salita — kilala. Ang Diyos ay hindi lamang makapangyarihan. Siya ay isang Persona — at kilala ka Niya. Ang Kanyang kaalaman tungkol sa iyo ay mas malalim pa kaysa sa kaalaman ng iyong pinaka-malapit na kaibigan. At ang tanong — kilala mo rin ba Siya?"

Movement 3 · TALAKAY
Deepen — Truth Becomes Personal
25 minutes · Teaching + Story + Reflection
▼
Part A — Ang Katotohanan (5 min · Slide 6)
Main Truth — Project & Say Twice
"Ang Diyos ay hindi isang ideya na dapat paniwalaan — Siya ay isang Persona na gustong makilala ka. At ito ay nangangahulugang ang pananampalataya ay hindi relihiyon — ito ay relasyon."
🎯 5 Minutes Only — One Idea
Project the main truth. Say it. Write it on a board. Then unpack ONE thing: the difference between knowing ABOUT someone and truly KNOWING them. Use Psalm 139 language: "He knows when you sit. When you rise. What you are thinking right now. That is not surveillance — that is intimacy." Stop. The story carries everything else.
Part B — Kwento ng Buhay (10 min · Your Story)
📖 Your Personal Story Framework
The Moment You Moved from Knowing ABOUT to Knowing
1. The Before: "Noon, ang Diyos para sa akin ay ___. Alam ko ang tungkol sa Kanya pero hindi ko Siya kilala dahil ___."

2. The Moment: Specific, dateable. Where were you? What happened? The more specific, the more it lands.

3. The Turning: "Sa sandaling iyon, napagtanto ko na ___."

4. The Difference: "Ngayon, ang Diyos para sa akin ay ___."

Under 8 minutes. Imperfect is more powerful than polished. Do not moralize at the end.
Part C — Reflection (10 min · Slide 7 · Participant Page)
📱 Participant Mobile Page — Individual Reflection
  1. Ask participants to open their phones and go to the participant page (project QR code)
  2. Project Slide 7 with three reflection questions
  3. 4 minutes of individual writing — real silence, you stay quiet
  4. Optional: "May gustong magbahagi? Isang pangungusap lamang." Receive 2–3 shares
🎯 The 4-Minute Silence — Do Not Break It
Filipino group culture is deeply uncomfortable with silence. Resist the urge to fill it. Walk slowly around the room. Your calm presence gives permission for genuine reflection. If someone cries — that is the Spirit. A quiet nod is enough. Do not interrupt.

Movement 4 · GAWIN
Practice — Do Something With the Truth
25 minutes · Practicum + EOLO Check-in
▼
Part A — Competence Practicum (15 min)
🙌 Partner Roleplay — "Kung Sasabihin Mo..."
Introducing a Personal God — Natural Conversation
Pairs within each table. Scenario: "Nakausap mo ang iyong kaibigan at nabanggit niya: 'Bakit ka ba lagi sa simbahan? Ano ba ang meron doon para sa iyo?' — Paano mo sasagutin nang natural, nang hindi mukhang nagse-sermon?"

Round 1 (3 min): Person A responds. Person B listens, then gives one affirmation: "Ang pinaka-natural na parte ay ___."

Round 2 (3 min): Switch roles.

Table debrief (5 min): Table Coach: "Sino ang natuklasan ng isang bagay na hindi pa nila nasasabi noon?" 1–2 shares per table.

You close with: "Hindi ninyo kailangan ng perpektong script. Kailangan lang ninyo ng tunay na karanasan at ang tapang na ibahagi ito."
Part B — EOLO Check-in (10 min · Slide 8 · Every Session)
🌏 EOLO Standing Check-in
  1. Everyone stands. Project Slide 8.
  2. You: "Sa Session 1 pa lang tayo — pero gusto kong itanong na: mayroon ba kayong isang tao sa isip ninyo? Isang tao sa inyong buhay na hindi pa nakakakilala kay Jesus?"
  3. Each table: go around — everyone says ONE name or "wala pa ako" honestly. No judgment.
  4. Table Coach leads 60-second specific prayer per table for the names mentioned
  5. Participants update EOLO section on their participant page
🎯 Session 1 EOLO Note
Some will not have a name yet — especially newer believers. That is fine and expected. Plant the question: "Who is the one person?" By Session 3 most will have a name. By Session 6 most will have had a conversation. This check-in is cumulative — it builds every session.

Movement 5 · DALHIN SA BAHAY
Send — Formation Continues at Home
10 minutes · Assignment + Verse + Pangako Ko + Closing
▼
The Assignment (Slides 10–11)
🏠 Takdang-Aralin — 10 Araw
Mag-usap sa Diyos — Isang Pangungusap Bawat Gabi
"Sa susunod na sampung araw — bago matulog — magsulat ng isang pangungusap. Hindi pormal na panalangin. Isang pangungusap lamang:

'Diyos, ngayong araw naramdaman ko ___.'  ·  'Diyos, hindi ko maintindihan ang ___.'  ·  'Diyos, salamat sa ___.'

Sa susunod na session, ang unang tanong: 'Ilang araw kang nakapagsulat?' Walang shame — datos lang."
Memory Verse Drill (Slide 9)
📝 Talata ng Memorya · Session 1
Jeremias 29:13
"Hahanapin ninyo Ako at mahahanap Ako, kapag hinahanap ninyo Ako nang buong puso."
📝 Look-Say-Cover-Check Drill
  1. Everyone reads from screen — twice together
  2. Screen goes blank — say from memory together
  3. Pairs say it to each other without looking
  4. Screenshot Slide 9 or distribute physical cards
Pangako Ko (Slide 10)
🎤 Lead this — no rushing
"Bago tayo mag-close, gusto kong makuha ang inyong pangako. Sa inyong participant page o journal: 'Sa susunod na dalawang linggo, isasabuhay ko ang katotohanang kilala ako ng Diyos sa pamamagitan ng _______________.' Isang bagay. Specific. Magagawa mo talaga."
The Closing Ritual — Same Every Session
🕊 Closing · 3 Minutes · Slide 12
  1. Everyone stands
  2. Memory verse together from memory — final time
  3. You pray specifically — name 2–3 participants and their EOLO person
  4. Everyone together, hands raised: "Disciples who make disciples."
  5. Table Coaches dismiss their tables — remind of assignment and next session date
📋 Post-Session Intern Debrief — 10 min
Three questions only: (1) "Sino ang pinaka-memorable na kalahok ngayon — bakit?" (2) "Anong momento ang pinaka-makapangyarihan sa inyong mesa?" (3) "Anong pastoral concern ang kailangan nating bantayan?" Write down names. Pray together. These feed into Session 2 planning.
